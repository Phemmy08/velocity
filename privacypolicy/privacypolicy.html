ONLINE PRIVACY POLICY

This Online Privacy Policy describes how Velocity Flex ("VFX", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information you provide when using our online services. By using this website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

This policy is effective as of February 15, 2024, and is intended for general guidance. VFX reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage you to periodically review this page to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

All provisions of this policy, "Client Information" refers to all personally identifiable information related to a particular individual, including (but not restricted to) name, address, telephone, fax, and e-mail addresses, and is not limited to:

  • Mailing addresses
  • E-mail addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Other information that can be used to identify, contact, or locate an individual

In using this Website, you consent to collect both electronically through this website.

INFORMATION COLLECTED BY VELOCITY FLEX ("VFX")

VFX collects personal information through various channels to support recruitment, employment, communications, and service delivery. The types of information we collect include:

1. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE DIRECTLY

We collect personal information directly from you in several situations, including when you:

  • Apply for a job with VFX or one of our recruiting clients
  • Register for a job posting
  • Complete forms related to our recruitment or job coordination
  • Create an account on our website or platform
  • Subscribe to communications from us
  • Contact us with questions or requests
  • Participate in onboarding or employment-related activities

Information we may collect includes, but not limited to your contact details, work history, education, benefit selections, and other employment-related data.

2. INFORMATION FROM THIRD-PARTY SOURCES

We may also obtain personal information about you from trusted third-party sources, including:

  • Background check providers
  • Consumer reporting agencies
  • Employment eligibility verification services
  • Professional references listed on your application
  • Publicly available sources (e.g., professional networking sites)

3. AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED INFORMATION

When you visit VFX websites or use our online services, we or our authorized service providers may automatically collect technical data such as your browser information and search terms:

  • Internet protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of access
  • Referring website or pages viewed

This information is collected through standard technologies such as cookies, server logs, and similar tools, and is used to help us understand usage patterns, maintain system security, and improve our services. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

USE OF INFORMATION BY VFX

We use your information to provide you with the best possible online experience related to your employment or client relationships with us. We always strive to be clear about how personal information is used.

We implement every precaution be used for the purpose including, but not limited to:

1. COMMUNICATION

We use information to coordinate your employment, your relationship as a client, or to share important updates. Occasionally, we may also reach out about products or services offered by VFX or our marketing third-party affiliates. You will have the option to opt out of these promotional communications at any time.

2. IP ADDRESS COLLECTION

Our technical systems automatically assigned to your device. In some cases, IP addresses may be linked with other data and considered personal information. We use IP addresses to help diagnose problems with our server and to collect personal demographic information for reporting purposes and to assist us in providing personalized services.

3. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing and personalize your experience. For example, cookies can allow certain forms to be prefilled with previously submitted information. Some of these technologies let us personalize content throughout our website when you return.

Most web browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that opting out may impact the site's functionality.

MINOR INFORMATION – NOT INTENDED USERS

VFX does not knowingly or intentionally employ, recruit, collect information from individuals under the age of 16. If you are under 16 years old, please do not use our website or provide us any information, as we are not knowingly collect personal information from anyone in this age group.

Although VFX does not specifically target individuals below the age of 18, some clients may request us to provide recruiting services for entry-level positions. We limit our communications in compliance with applicable laws.

We do VFX does not knowingly collect information from, communicate with, or seek to enroll anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that personal information from a child under 16 has been collected without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

We do not knowingly distribute honor or market our services to children.

VFX is committed to safeguarding your personal information and will never share your information under the following circumstances:

1. THIRD-PARTY SERVICE PROVIDERS

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party contractors and partners to provide related services such as payroll, benefits, tax support, payroll processing, or background screening. These providers are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information and are prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than to provide designated services. All confidentiality and access, we impose opt-out text messages so the contract will be relevant under our privacy policy affiliate.

2. BUSINESS EMPLOYMENT

Personal and professional information may be shared with your employer, employers, and services in the context of employment relationships, benefits, associated technology requirements.

3. LEGAL AND REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

We may disclose personal information when required by law or to respond to valid legal processes, such as a search warrant, subpoena, or court order, or to respond from governmental agencies.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of all or a portion of VFX's assets or business, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place during such transfers.

4. EMERGENCY PROVIDERS

To authorized emergency benefits, we may share relevant personal information with third-party contractors as necessary for legally required notices or for benefit management purposes or any applicable law limiting such information sharing.
